Overnight Oats

Ingredientes

  • 3 cucharadas avena
  • c/n Leche
  • 1 cucharita semillas de chía
  • Canela y edulcorante a gusto
  • 1 banana
  • 2 cucharadas grandes yogurt
  • 1 tapita esencia de vainilla
  • Frutos secos (almendras y pasas de uva)

Instrucciones

  1. rolled oats, one tablespoon chia seeds, maple syrup and vanilla in a glass jar, bowl or container.
  2. Pour over the milk, place the lid on and shake so that it's well combined and there are no clumps of chia seeds. If using a bowl, mix well with a spoon
  3. Allow it to set in the fridge for 2 - 4 hours or overnight
  4. Top with your favourite toppings such as fresh fruit and granola and enjoy!
